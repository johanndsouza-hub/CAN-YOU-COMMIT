The problem

Most people don't struggle to start.

The problem is follow-through. COMMIT makes it visible — to yourself and the people who matter. The streak isn't the goal. It's the evidence that you kept your word.

Motivation is unreliable.

It gets you started. It doesn't keep you going. Most people don't fail loudly — they just quietly stop.

Accountability is reliable.

A visible streak makes stopping a conscious choice. That friction is the whole point. Results are the by-product of consistency.
