The problem

Most goals do not fail loudly. They disappear quietly.

Commit is designed for the moment where motivation fades and visibility has to take over.

Habit apps track activity.

Charts, counters, reminders, and dashboards can become noise. They show data, but they do not always create accountability.

Commit apps protect promises.

The point is not another metric. The point is proof that you showed up, and a simple system that makes stopping harder to ignore.
