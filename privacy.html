COMMIT — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

COMMIT ("we", "us", "the app") helps you build commitment through daily streaks. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. Questions: support@canyoucommit.app.

1. Information we collect

  • Account: email, display name, avatar (Google, Apple, or email sign-in).
  • Activity: commitments/streaks you create, check-ins, reflection notes.
  • Social: friend connections and shared-streak status.
  • Purchases: Streak Saver purchase records (processed by Apple or Stripe; we never receive card details).
  • Technical: basic device and app-version info.

2. How we use it

To provide and sync your streaks, enable friends and shared streaks, send notifications you enable, process purchases, and keep the service secure. We do not sell your data or use it for cross-app advertising or tracking.

3. Legal bases (GDPR)

Performance of our contract, your consent (notifications), and our legitimate interest in operating and securing the service.

4. Service providers

Google Firebase (auth, database, hosting), Apple (sign-in, purchases, notifications), Stripe (web payments). Data is stored on Google Cloud.

Google Analytics 4 is used on the canyoucommit.app marketing website to measure page traffic, referral sources, and CTA clicks. No account data, email, display name, habit content, or personal information is sent to Google Analytics. Google Analytics is not active inside the COMMIT app itself.

5. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, streaks, check-ins, and friendships.

6. Your rights

You can access, export, and delete your data anytime in Settings → Privacy & Data. Under EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA/CPRA, and Canada PIPEDA / Quebec Law 25 you may have further rights. Contact us to exercise them.

7. Security

Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest on Google Cloud, with access restricted by authentication and security rules.

8. Children

COMMIT is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country, up to 16 in parts of the EU).

9. International transfers

Your data may be processed in other countries (e.g. United States) by our providers, under appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the date above; material changes are notified in-app.

11. Contact

support@canyoucommit.app
