Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open COMMIT, go to Settings, tap Privacy & Data, then Delete My Account. This permanently removes all your data.

How do I export my data?

Settings → Privacy & Data → Export My Data. You'll get a JSON file with all your streaks and check-ins.

I made a purchase and something went wrong

Email us at support@canyoucommit.app with your Apple ID and a description of the issue. For Apple IAP refunds, you can also contact Apple Support directly at reportaproblem.apple.com.

My streak looks wrong

Pull down to refresh the home screen. If it still looks off, email us with your account email and we'll look into it.
